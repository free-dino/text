> [!Lemma] Lemma
> If $h$ is a positive number and $n$ a positive integer, then $$(1+h)^n \ge 1 + nh$$
> This is the trivial consequence of the binomial theorem 

The stronger inequality: $$(1+h)^n \ge 1 + nh + \frac {n(n-1)}{2}h^2$$
